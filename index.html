Welcome to RipVIS

RipVIS is a large-scale video instance segmentation benchmark for rip current detection, accepted at CVPR 2025. Our goal is to advance beach safety by providing a comprehensive dataset and benchmark for researchers and practitioners.

The full dataset, baseline models, and results will be released with the camera-ready paper in 2025. Stay tuned for updates!
