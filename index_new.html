RipVIS

Rip Currents Video Instance Segmentation Benchmark
for Beach Monitoring and Safety

Accepted at CVPR 2025

RipVIS is a large-scale video instance segmentation benchmark for detecting rip currents from real-world beach footage. It includes tasks such as classification, bounding box detection, oriented bounding boxes, semantic segmentation, and instance segmentation.

Multi-source: drones, phones, fixed cams
1 class: rip current (multi-instance)
